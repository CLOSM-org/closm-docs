## Phone Cases

High-quality polycarbonate hard cases.

### Features

- Excellent impact resistance
- Lightweight and comfortable
- UV printing for vivid colors
- Wireless charging compatible

### Supported Models

Varies by shipping country. Major manufacturers (Apple iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, etc.) and recent models are supported.

Select your shipping country during creation to see available models.
