## Manufacturing Partner

CLOSM i merchandise is produced and shipped by **[Printful](https://www.printful.com/)**.

Printful is one of the world's largest Print on Demand services.

---

## Order to Delivery Flow

```
Order Confirmed → Production → Quality Check → Shipping → Delivery
       │              │            │              │           │
       └─ Immediate ──┴─ 2-5 days ─┴─── 1 day ────┴─ Varies ──┘
```

---

## Estimated Delivery Times

| Region | Production | Shipping | Total |
|:-------|:----------:|:--------:|:------|
| USA | 2-5 business days | 3-8 business days | **5-13 business days** |
| Canada | 2-5 business days | 5-15 business days | 7-20 business days |
| Europe | 2-5 business days | 5-20 business days | 7-25 business days |
| Japan | 2-5 business days | 5-10 business days | 7-15 business days |
| Other | 2-5 business days | 10-30 business days | 12-35 business days |

:::note[Peak Seasons]
Delivery may take longer during holidays.
:::
