This guide walks you through creating your first fractal art on CLOSM i.

## 1. Create an Account

1. Visit [CLOSM i](https://i.closm.llc)
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Register with email or social account

## 2. Create Your Artwork

1. After logging in, click the "Create" button
2. The canvas will appear

### Basic Controls

- **Mouse drag**: Move the view
- **Scroll**: Zoom in/out
- **Click**: Zoom in centered on that point

### Adjust Parameters

In the right panel, you can adjust:

- **Position (x, y)**: Which part of the fractal to display
- **Zoom**: Magnification level
- **Iterations**: Calculation precision (higher = more detail)

## 3. Set Colors

Configure colors using the color bar:

1. Click on the color bar to add colors
2. Drag colors to adjust positions
3. Double-click colors to change them

## 4. Save Your Work

1. Click the "Save" button
2. Enter title and description
3. Choose visibility (public/private)
4. Click "Save" to finish

Congratulations! Your first artwork is complete.
