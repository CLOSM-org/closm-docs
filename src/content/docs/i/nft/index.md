You can record your fractal art as **NFT (Non-Fungible Token)** on the blockchain.

By making your artwork an NFT, ownership is verified on the blockchain, and it becomes viewable and tradeable on marketplaces like OpenSea.

## Requirements

- Logged into CLOSM i
- Have at least one artwork
- Crypto wallet connected
- **Lite** or **Premium** plan (Free plan cannot mint)

---

## Quick Start

1. Set up a MetaMask wallet
2. Connect wallet on the NFT page
3. Select artwork to mint
4. Choose chain (Polygon/Ethereum)
5. Execute mint and approve in wallet
6. After completion, verify on OpenSea
