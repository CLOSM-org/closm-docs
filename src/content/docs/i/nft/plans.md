NFT minting availability and monthly limits vary by subscription plan.

## Features by Plan

| Feature | Free | Lite | Premium |
|---------|------|------|---------|
| NFT minting | - | Yes | Yes |
| Polygon | - | Yes | Yes |
| Ethereum | - | - | Yes |
| Monthly mints (Polygon) | 0 | 3 | 10 |
| Monthly mints (Ethereum) | 0 | 0 | 3 |

:::note
Free plan users can view NFT feature explanations but cannot mint.
:::

---

## Monthly Mint Limits

### Reset Timing

Monthly mint counts reset on the **1st of each month**.

### Checking Usage

Check in the "Usage Status" section on the NFT page:

- Current month's mints (Polygon / Ethereum separately)
- Remaining count
- Reset date and time

### Rollover

Unused counts **do not roll over** to the next month. They reset monthly.

## Pricing Structure

### Cost of NFT Minting

There is **no additional CLOSM i fee** for NFT minting.

What you need:

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Subscription | Lite or Premium plan |
| Gas fees | Blockchain transaction fees (MATIC/ETH) |

### Gas Fee Estimates

| Chain | Normal | Congested |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| Polygon | 0.01–0.05 MATIC | ~0.1 MATIC |
| Ethereum | 0.001–0.005 ETH | 0.01+ ETH |

:::tip
Gas fees fluctuate with network congestion. Check the actual estimate on the NFT page before minting.
:::

## Recommended Usage by Plan

### Lite Plan

Make the most of **3 mints/month (Polygon)**:

- Mint your best selected works
- Low-cost minting on Polygon
- Great for trying out NFT features

### Premium Plan

Leverage **10 mints/month (Polygon) + 3 mints/month (Ethereum)**:

- Mint many artworks

## Upgrading Plans

### Lite → Premium

Upgrade from the plan settings page:

- Ethereum minting becomes available immediately
- Polygon monthly limit increases (3 → 10)

### Free → Lite/Premium

Upgrading from Free unlocks NFT minting features.

→ [Plan details & changes](/i/account/)

## Common Questions

### Q: If I upgrade mid-month, what happens to my limits?

The new plan's limits apply immediately. Already used counts remain counted.

### Q: What happens when I reach the limit?

You cannot mint until the next month's reset on the 1st. There is no option to purchase additional mints.

### Q: What if I downgrade?

Already minted NFTs are not affected. From the next month, the new plan's limits apply.
