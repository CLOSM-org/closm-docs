The CLOSM i gallery lets you browse fractal art created by artists worldwide.

## Accessing the Gallery

Click "Gallery" in the header.

## Viewing Artworks

### Display Modes

- **Grid view**: Thumbnails in a list
- **Detail view**: Large artwork display

### Sort Order

- **Newest**: Most recently published first
- **Popular**: Most favorites first

## Search and Filter

### Keyword Search

Search by entering keywords:
- Artwork title
- Description
- Tags
- Artist name

### Filters

- **Period**: Today, this week, this month, all time
- **Tags**: Filter by specific tags

## Artwork Detail Page

Click an artwork to open its detail page.

### Information Displayed

- Artwork image (high resolution)
- Title and description
- Artist information
- Parameters (x, y, zoom, etc.)
- Links to original/derivative works

### Actions

- **Favorite**: Click the heart icon
- **Create from this**: Make a derivative
- **Create merchandise**: Make phone cases etc.
- **Share**: Share on social media

## Artist Page

Click an artist's name to see their:
- Profile
- Public artworks
- Follower count

## Follow Feature

Follow your favorite artists to stay updated on their new works.

1. Open the artist's page
2. Click "Follow"

Check followed artists on your profile page.
